Core Technologies

Our windshield-free design relies on three revolutionary technologies that work together to create a seamless driving experience.

Panoramic AR Dome

The roof and windshield area are composed of a single piece of smart glass that displays the external environment, navigation information, and social content. It can adjust transparency as needed to create a private space.

Sensor Fusion & AI

Sensors throughout the vehicle (lidar, radar, cameras) create a 360-degree environment model far beyond human vision. AI is responsible for path planning and hazard warning.

By-wire Gesture Control

Traditional steering wheel is eliminated. Driving mode switching and precise control are performed through gestures, voice, and touch panels.
